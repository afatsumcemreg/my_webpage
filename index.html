About Me

Hello! Welcome to my webpage. My name is Mustafa Germec and I am 32 years old. I am from Turkey. I got the title of Food Engineer from the Akdeniz University, Faculty of Engineering, Department of Food Engineering in 2012, and after my BSc graduation, I have completed my education of Master's in 2014 and Doctorate in 2019 at the same department. Between April 2013 and December 2013, I have worked as a Food Engineer in the food sector. Followed by, I have worked as a Research Assistant at the Akdeniz University, Department of Food Engineering in 2013-2017. After I have lost my job at the university, I have worked as a Food Engineer in a Catering Company in Sakarya, Turkey from September 2018-March 2019. I am married and have a Son. Now, I am living in Luzern, Switzerland with my family. On this webpage, you will find more information about me. This webpage includes my information, contact information, hobbies, languages I spoke, educations, experiences, publications including theses, SCI articles, non-SCI Articles, translates, conference proceedings, certificates, projects, and some photos.

On the other hand, nowadays I am learning German, writing my scientific articles, and reviewing the scientific articles. Besides, I am interested in programming, data science, and web development. Therefore, in my free time, I am learning programming languages(Python and JavaScript), HTML, CSS, SQL, and data science. The purpose of my learning is to integrate them with my area of expertise (production, purification, and characterization of value-added products, pretreatment processes of renewable resources, and statistical, kinetical, and mathematical modeling of fermentation processes), train myself, and find a good job in Switzerland. I hope I find a good job in the direction of my targets.
